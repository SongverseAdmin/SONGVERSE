## 1. 定位 / Positioning

中文：
本体系为中国音乐人、创作者、团队及机构提供一套标准化、可落地、可复制的音乐海外发行与推广流程。本体系面向全球市场，并以欧美市场作为核心参考场景（同时兼容全球市场）。

English:
This standard provides a standardized, implementable, and replicable workflow for Chinese musicians, creators, teams, and institutions to distribute and promote music internationally, using Europe and North America as the primary reference markets while remaining globally applicable.

---

## 2. 核心原则 / Core Principles

- 立足中国，面向全球  
  Rooted in China, for global markets.

- 专业发行，全平台覆盖  
  Professional distribution with broad platform coverage.

- 海外推广，精准运营  
  Overseas marketing with targeted, iterative operations.

- 流程透明，合规安全  
  Transparent, compliant, and safe processes.

- 标准服务，长期稳定  
  Standardized delivery and long-term stability.

---

## 3. 核心理念 / Core Ideas

- 标准化路径：为中国音乐提供标准化海外发行路径  
  Standardized path for international distribution.

- 全球分发：作品上架全球主流音乐平台  
  Global distribution to major DSPs.

- 市场聚焦：聚焦全球推广，以欧美市场为核心参考  
  Market focus with EU/NA as core reference.

- 透明追溯：全流程透明可追溯  
  End-to-end transparency and traceability.

- 数据监测：专业海外数据监测与优化  
  Data monitoring and optimization.

- 合规结算：安全规范的海外收益结算  
  Compliant revenue settlement and reconciliation.

---

## 4. 标准流程 / Standard Process

中文：
SONGVERSE 标准覆盖从准备到长期服务支持的完整生命周期：

English:
SONGVERSE covers the full lifecycle from preparation to long-term operations:

- PREPARE：作品与资料标准化整理（Music & materials preparation）
- DISTRIBUTE：全球音乐平台正式上架（Global distribution / release）
- PROMOTE：全球推广（以欧美市场为主）（Global promotion with EU/NA focus）
- MONITOR：数据监测与效果优化（Monitoring & optimization）
- REVENUE：海外收益结算与对账（Revenue settlement & reconciliation）
- SERVICE：长期海外运营支持（Ongoing operations support）

---

## 5. 适用对象 / Applicable Objects

- 希望出海发行的中国音乐人、歌手、创作者  
  Chinese artists/creators who plan to distribute globally.

- 需要全球推广（欧美为主）的音乐作品与团队  
  Works/teams seeking global marketing, especially EU/NA.

- 追求标准化海外服务的机构与个人  
  Organizations/individuals seeking standardized, auditable delivery.

---

## 6. 关于本体系 / About SONGVERSE

中文：
本体系为开源实践版，专注于中国音乐海外发行与全球推广的标准化，未来将持续迭代与优化。

English:
This is an open-source practice version focused on standardizing overseas distribution and global promotion for Chinese music, and it will be continuously updated and improved.
